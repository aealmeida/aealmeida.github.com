Imagine Boston 2030

May 2015

About Project

Imagine Boston 2030 is an awareness campaign that raises awarness to specific issues within Boston and how we can improve them for the future. I had to create two posters that worked both together and separately and raised awareness to one of these Boston issues. The issue I focused on is the relationship between art and graffitti in Boston.

Graffiti is often given a bad rep, but Boston actually has a vast amount of beautiful street art and graffitti. We know that Boston is known for its multiple art museums and galleries, but I feel that those arent the only places to appreciate art in this busy city.
